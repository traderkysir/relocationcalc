About RelocationCalc

Free tools for anyone planning an international move.

What Is RelocationCalc?

RelocationCalc.com is a free collection of 24 calculators and converters designed specifically for expats, digital nomads, and anyone planning an international relocation. All tools run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers, no account is required, and there's no paywall.

Our Tools

We cover four categories: financial planning tools (currency, cost of living, taxes, visas), health tools (BMI, insurance estimates, due dates), productivity tools (time zones, days tracking, date calculations), and unit converters (length, weight, temperature, clothing sizes).

Why Free?

This site is supported by display advertising. Keeping tools free and easy to use is our priority. We don't collect your data, require logins, or charge for any calculator.

Disclaimer

All calculators provide estimates only. Financial, tax, and visa-related results should be verified with qualified professionals. Exchange rates and cost-of-living data change frequently — always confirm current figures before making major decisions.
