Why Clothing Sizes Differ Internationally

Clothing sizes are not standardized globally. A US size 8 dress corresponds roughly to EU 38, UK 12, and AU 12 — but sizing can vary by brand. When shopping abroad, always check measurements in centimeters and use this chart as a starting point.
