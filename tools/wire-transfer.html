How to Minimize Wire Transfer Costs

For large relocation transfers ($10,000+), use a specialist like Wise, OFX, or Currencies Direct. Banks typically add 2–4% above the mid-market rate, which is invisible but very real. Always compare the total cost including fees AND the exchange rate spread.
