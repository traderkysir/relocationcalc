Understanding BMI Categories

BMI is a screening tool, not a definitive health measure. It doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or fat distribution. Use it as one data point alongside other health metrics.

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obese Class I: BMI 30–34.9
  • Obese Class II: BMI 35–39.9
  • Obese Class III: BMI 40+
