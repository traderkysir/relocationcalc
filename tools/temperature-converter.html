Temperature Conversion Formulas

°F to °C: (°F − 32) × 5/9  |  °C to °F: (°C × 9/5) + 32  |  °C to K: °C + 273.15

A quick trick: to roughly convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, double the Celsius figure and add 30 (e.g., 20°C ≈ 70°F). For exact values, use the calculator above.
