FCL vs LCL: Which Is Right for Your Move?

A 20ft container holds roughly 25–28 CBM — about the contents of a 2-bedroom apartment. A 40ft container holds 55–58 CBM. If you have less than 15 CBM, LCL (shared container) is usually more economical. For a full household move, FCL gives you better security and often better value.

  • LCL: Good for small volumes. Your goods share a container with others.
  • 20ft FCL: Best for 1–2 bedroom households moving internationally.
  • 40ft FCL: Best for 3+ bedroom households or office moves.
