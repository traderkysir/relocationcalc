Shoe Sizing Systems Explained

EU sizing is based on the length of the last (the mold) in Paris points (⅔ cm). US sizing is based on barleycorns (⅓ inch). UK sizing is similar to US but typically 1 size smaller for men's and 2 sizes smaller for women's.
