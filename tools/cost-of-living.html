Understanding Cost of Living Indexes

A cost of living index of 45 means life in that city costs roughly 45% of what it costs in a city indexed at 100. This tool uses your actual expenses as the baseline, making the comparison personal and actionable rather than abstract.

Most Popular Expat Destinations by Cost

  • Portugal (Lisbon/Madeira): 40–50% of major US/UK cities. Popular D7 visa destination.
  • Mexico City: 30–40% of New York. Large expat community, fast internet, great food scene.
  • Thailand: 25–35% of Western cities. Excellent healthcare, warm weather, strong expat infrastructure.
  • Colombia (Medellín): 30–40% of US cities. Nicknamed "City of Eternal Spring."
