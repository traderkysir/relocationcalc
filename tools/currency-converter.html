How to Use the Currency Converter

Enter the amount you want to convert, select your source currency, and choose your target currency. Click "Convert Now" to see the live result. Use the ⇄ button to instantly swap the two currencies.

Why Exchange Rates Matter for Expats

When moving abroad, even a 1–2% difference in the exchange rate on a $50,000 transfer can mean $500–$1,000 lost. Always compare the mid-market rate (what this tool shows) against what your bank offers — the difference is called the "spread" and is how banks and services make money.

  • Wise (formerly TransferWise) typically offers rates very close to mid-market
  • Bank wire transfers often add 2–4% above mid-market
  • Western Union varies — check the total cost including fees
