Portugal D7 Visa Requirements (2024)

The D7 "Passive Income Visa" allows non-EU citizens to live in Portugal if they have sufficient passive income. Key requirements include:

  • Minimum €820/month for the main applicant (2024 minimum wage)
  • Additional €246/month per adult dependent (30% of minimum wage)
  • Additional €164/month per minor dependent (20% of minimum wage)
  • Valid travel insurance and clean criminal record
  • Proof of accommodation in Portugal
  • Bank savings are helpful but not strictly required

Madeira and the Azores sometimes offer additional incentives for new residents. Consult a licensed Portuguese immigration attorney for current requirements.
