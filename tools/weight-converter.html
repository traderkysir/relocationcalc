Weight Units Around the World

The UK still commonly uses stones for body weight (1 stone = 14 pounds = 6.35 kg), while most of Europe uses kilograms. In the US, pounds are standard. Understanding these differences is important for medical forms, shipping, and grocery shopping abroad.
