Buying vs Renting Abroad: Key Considerations

The rent-vs-buy calculus changes dramatically outside your home country. Factors that make buying more attractive abroad: lower property prices, strong appreciation in growth markets, and stable long-term residency plans. Factors that favor renting: residency uncertainty, foreign buyer restrictions, and limited access to favorable mortgage rates as a non-citizen.
